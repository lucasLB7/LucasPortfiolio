Sabaa Engineering

Location: Ethiopia, Wereta


Duration: 3 months


Position: intern


Date: 2016

I was invited to participate in a water drilling operation for the government owned company Sabaa Engineering in the remote village Wereta, bordering lake Tana.

The opperation was designed to assist the local population to have easy access to clean water.

My position as an intern consisted of learning how to opperate drilling rigs, driving the team across the difficult terrain and handling basic relations between the villagers and our team.

De RattevalleBrug

Location: Belgium, Nieuwpoort


Duration: 1 month


Position: Summer job


Date: 2014

It is customary for students in Belgium to take up summer jobs.

As I have always enjoyed cooking and serving people I decided to work at a small local bistro, De RattevalleBrug.

It is located in a quiet village in Flanders, close to the Ocean.


For the first week I was on dishwash duty.. lol. After that I "graduated" to sous-chef and assisted in dressing the dishes, decorating plates and preparing basic meals.
