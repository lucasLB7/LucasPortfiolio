Sabaa Engineering

Location: Ethiopia, Wereta


Duration: 3 months


Position: intern


Date: 2016

I was invited to participate in a water drilling operation for the government owned company Sabaa Engineering in the remote village Wereta, bordering lake Tana.

The opperation was designed to assist the local population to have easy access to clean water.

My position as an intern consisted of learning how to opperate drilling rigs, driving the team across the difficult terrain and handling basic relations between the villagers and our team.

Vermont Flowers

Location: Kenya, Nairobi


Duration: 3 years


Position: Product manager


Date: 2014 - 2016

I was employed as the product manager for the new cosmetics division that Vermont Flowers was placing focus on.

Vermont Flowers is the world leader in floral preservation, located in the EPZ zone, they only deal with export, mainly to Europe, Russia, USA and Japan.

I was tasked with reserching the materials and know how to manufacture flower based perfumes, soaps and scent diffusers.


When the company was sold to the current management, I was releived of my position although I was succesful in creating the product line.

Decomagna ltd

Location: Kenya, Nairobi


Duration: 3 years


Position: Managing Director


Date: 2016 - present

In 2016 I negotiated an exclusivity agreement with Quick-Step to supply their materials in Kenya and East-Africa.

Quick-Step is the world leader in flooring products, with their headquarters in Wielsbeke, Belgium.

Since the inception of Decomagna ltd, we are now the largest supplier of laminate flooring, engineered wood and vinyls in Kenya.

We are in partnership with the Design Gallery, as division of Victoria Furniture, and cater to all markets (Retail, Wholesale and agency).

Our strategy has always been to provide our customers with a great service, great prices and full transparency.
